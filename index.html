Dr. Muhammad Yunus envisions a "New Bangladesh" built on principles of inclusivity, sustainability, and empowerment. His vision emphasizes the elimination of poverty, the creation of a socially conscious economy driven by social businesses, and fostering entrepreneurship at every level. He believes in unleashing human creativity, promoting youth leadership, and transitioning towards a circular economy to create a world of zero poverty, zero unemployment, and zero net carbon emissions. This vision reflects his commitment to transforming Bangladesh into a model for equitable and sustainable development
